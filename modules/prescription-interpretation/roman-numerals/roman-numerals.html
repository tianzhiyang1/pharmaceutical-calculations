How to Play:

  1. Half of the cards show numbers (1-100).
  2. The other half show their Roman numeral equivalents (I-C).
  3. Click on any card first - it will turn dark blue.
  4. Then click on its matching pair (number with Roman numeral).
  5. Correct matches turn green and score points!
  6. Incorrect pairs flash red briefly.
  7. Match all pairs to finish.
