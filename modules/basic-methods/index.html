Example 1:

If two capsules contains 600 mg of Benadryl, how many milligrams of Benadryl would be contained in 10 capsules?

Step 1: Identify what you know and what you need to find
  1. Identify the unknown unit of measurement.
  2. Find the known equivalent.

Unknown: X mg of Benadryl in 10 capsules

Known equivalent: 600 mg of Benadryl in 2 capsules

Step 2: Set up the proportion
  1. Write the known equivalent in a fraction on the left side of the equation.
  2. Write the unknown desired equivalent in a fraction on the right side of the equation, using X for the unknown value.
  3. Label the units so that the like unit is placed the same position within each ratio. This sets ratios that are equivalent to each other.
