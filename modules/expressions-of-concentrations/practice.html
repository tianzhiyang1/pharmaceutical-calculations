Percentage Strength

+

Steps to solve percentage strength problems:

  1. Identify the type of percentage
    • w/v (weight in volume)
    • v/v (volume in volume)
    • w/w (weight in weight)
  2. Convert all units to standard units, ie, mL for volume; g for weight
  3. Set up the ratio and proportion by using the format:
    Amount of drug
    Total amount (mL or g)
    =
    x
    100
  4. Solve for the unknown

Ratio Strength

+

Steps to solve ratio strength problems:

  1. Identify the type of ratio strength
    • w/v: g in mL (eg, 1 g in 1000 mL)
    • v/v: mL in mL (eg, 1 mL in 1000 mL)
    • w/w: g in g (eg, 1 g in 1000 g)
  2. Convert all units to standard units, ie, mL for volume; g for weight
  3. Set up the ratio and proportion by using the format:
    Amount of drug
    Total amount (mL or g)
    =
    1
    x
  4. Solve for the unknown
